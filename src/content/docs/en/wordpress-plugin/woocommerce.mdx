---
title: 'WooCommerce'
description: 'WooCommerce support.'
---

The Procaptcha plugin supports the following [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) forms:

**1. Custom account forms**

The [WooCommerce account page](https://woocommerce.com/document/the-my-account-page/) contains custom Register, Login and Reset Password forms.   
To enable their protection visit the Procaptcha settings, open the `Core Forms` tab and toggle on the related switchers.

**2. Checkout forms**

Nowadays, WooCommerce includes 2 different checkouts: the classic one and the blocks one. The checkout page uses one of them depending on your theme.
The Procaptcha plugin supports both of them; to enable the protection visit the Procaptcha settings, open the `WooCommerce` tab and toggle on the related switcher.

**3. Order tracking form**

WooCommerce comes with the built-in [order tracking form shortcode](https://woocommerce.com/document/woocommerce-shortcodes/), which you can add to any page.
To enable the protection visit the Procaptcha settings, open the `WooCommerce` tab and toggle on the related switcher.
